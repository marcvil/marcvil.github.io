About Me

Hi!

I'm a Game Designer looking for a junior/entry level job or an intership in the games industry as a Game Designer. I'm based in Barcelona (Spain) and I'm willing to relocate.

I have a master in Molecular Biotechnology and a degree in Biotechnology (you can see more information on my LinkedIn). So the obvious question is: What are you doing here? Playing videogames and following the state of the games industry has been a constant in my whole life. This passion drove me to install Unity on the last year of University (bad idea, i didn't sleep for a week to finish my Uni thesis) and the love for creating games has been a constant since then. I'm currently studying a 2 year course on multiplatform app development at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya to continue improving while working.

I am self taught in all aspects of game development. I mainly use Unity and Blender but I also have the basics of Photoshop, Unreal Engine 4 and Substance Painter. I publish games under the Mappvi Games Company name. I designed the logo myself with Blender3D.
