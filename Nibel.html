Nibel

A First person Shooter with the power to teleport (dishonored franchise blink ability mimic)

Nibel is a game made by a team of 3 in 2 days for a Unity3D One week training. I could work closely with a programmer and we work closely to improve the abilities of the main character. It was a really good experience to be able to work with a team(as I don't have a circle of friends in the development scene) and focus on level design, polishing mechanichs and level art.

My Contribution

  • Decide the mechanichs that we wanted to focus on our game and cut other mechanichs to reach the deadline.
  • All the art and animations are made by me in Blender.
  • Work closely with the programmer to get the best animation for the bow.
  • Design the Menu and UI in the game.
