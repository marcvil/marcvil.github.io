Mi Poesia Es Un Juego

Narrative game based on Federico García Lorca's life.

You can play it on Itch.io

This is a solo game project made for the Cultural Game Jam, organized by DEV (Asociación Española de Empresas Productoras y Desarrolladoras de Videojuegos. The game was made in two days and the subject of the Jam was Spanish Culture and the Fair Use of Intelectual Property.

Mi Poesia Es Un Juego was one of the project winners (8 projects selected from more than 60 games) and it was exposed in a public event in Madrid. I received some valuable feedback from players and made some friends there.

A nice highlight from the trip was that I could attend to Greg Buchanan(Narrative designer in Metro Exodus, No Man's Sky, American Election and more) speech on Originality and Inspiration in Triple AAA and Indie development
