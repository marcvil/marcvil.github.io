Lightfinder

Modern take of the classic Snake for Android

You can play it on Android

This the first game I have ever done. I had some fun playing with Unity and I decided that if I wanted to learn and become a game developer I had to take smaller steps but developing projects from beginning to the end. So I decided to create a small game with simple mechanichs so I can learn how to polish with my friends' feedback, monetizing the game with ads, publishing on Google Play and see some statistics of my game.

Based on my friends' feedback I adjusted the player's and enemies speed and spawn points. one adjustment I had to take care was the collectibles spawn points. Initially, they appeared random all over the screen but after some adjustments, they only appear above the joysticks, so the player is always playing on the superior half of the screen.
